##### Our paper presented at NAPS is as follows:

* <a href="/papers/Hassan-MG-Sizing-CCCV/" class="off">Hassan Zahid Butt and Xingpeng Li, “Approximating CCCV charging using SOC-dependent tapered charging power constraints in long-term microgrid planning”, North American Power Symposium, Hartford, CT, USA, Oct. 2025.</a>
