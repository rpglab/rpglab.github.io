Dr. Jesus Silva-Rodriguez received his PhD degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Houston, Houston, TX, USA in December 2025. Jesus started working in the RPG lab first as an undergraduate research assistant in August 2020. After he completed his BS degree at UH, he then became a direct PhD student here in January 2021. Jesus is our first direct-PhD graduate.

Jesus worked in the RPG lab as a PhD student from Jan. 2021 to Dec. 2025. 

PhD Dissertation title: *"Decentralized Co-Optimization of Water and Energy Distribution Systems"*

First Job: Large Load Integration Engineer, Electric Reliability Council of Texas (ERCOT), Texas, USA.
